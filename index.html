Roof of Africa Rally

Experience the Legendary Roof of Africa Rally

The Mother of Hard Enduro in the Mountain Kingdom of Lesotho

Event Overview

The Roof of Africa Rally is one of the most famous off-road motorcycle racing events in Africa. It takes place every year in the mountain kingdom of Lesotho and attracts riders from many different countries.
The rally challenges riders to travel across difficult mountain terrain, rivers and rocky paths. Because of the extreme conditions, the event is often called the "Mother of Hard Enduro". Thousands of spectators and adventure tourists visit Lesotho to watch the race and experience the country's beautiful landscapes.

Discover What Makes the Roof of Africa Rally Special

  • Experience one of the toughest motorcycle races in the world
  • Explore the beautiful mountains and landscapes of Lesotho
  • Watch international riders compete in extreme conditions
  • Enjoy Basotho culture, food and local tourism activities
